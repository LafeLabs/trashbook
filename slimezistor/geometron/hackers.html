HACKERS

The role of hackers in Trash Magic is to hack electronics and computer technology found in waste streams into free and open systems we can use. This term is used not to refer to anything having to do with "security" but with the culture of tinkering with code in ways not intended by the creators of a product, and with the culture of unix/linux which is needed for making most modern computer systems work.

All that said, planned obsolescence is just as much an adversary as any other, and the methods of offensive hacking must be used to make devices manufactured by bad actors like Apple into usable technology.
